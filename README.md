## 📋 Features Implemented

### Core Features
- ✅ **Salary Table** - Search, filter, sort, and paginate through salary records
- ✅ **Company Pages** - View company details, median compensation, and level distribution
- ✅ **Compare** - Side-by-side salary comparison with delta calculations
- ✅ **Reviews** - Anonymous employee reviews with ratings
- ✅ **Interviews** - Real interview experiences and questions
- ✅ **Tools** - Salary calculator, hike calculator, equity calculator
- ✅ **Community** - Company discussions and topics
- ✅ **Workplace Index** - Company rankings on culture, compensation, growth

### Authentication
- ✅ User Registration with full name, email, company, role
- ✅ User Login with email and password
- ✅ Protected routes (requires login to access)
- ✅ Session management using localStorage and cookies

### Design
- ✅ Clean White & Green theme
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile-first)
- ✅ Glass-morphism cards and badges
- ✅ Fixed background image on hero section
- ✅ Consistent UI across all pages
